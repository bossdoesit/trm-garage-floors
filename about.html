Who We Are

TRM Garage Floors

TRM Garage Floors is an owner-operated concrete coating company based in the East Valley. We specialize in 100% polyaspartic floor coatings for residential garages, patios, decks, and commercial spaces across Gilbert, Mesa, Chandler, Queen Creek, San Tan Valley, Scottsdale, and the surrounding area.

Our mission is straightforward: provide homeowners and business owners with floor coating systems that are genuinely superior to the basic epoxy products most contractors still install. We use 100% polyaspartic chemistry on every job because it handles Arizona's heat and UV environment better, cures faster, and holds up longer. Not because it's the easiest product to apply, but because it's the right one for this market.

Why 100% Polyaspartic

Most floor coating contractors in the Valley use epoxy as a base coat because it's cheaper and easier to install. The result looks fine on day one. The problem shows up in year two or three, when the UV exposure and extreme surface heat that Arizona garages experience starts breaking down the coating from the outside in.

Polyaspartic was developed as a coating chemistry that handles what epoxy can't. It's UV stable — it won't yellow under Arizona's intense sun. It cures in hours instead of days, which is how we complete most residential garage floors in a single day. It's flexible enough to move with the concrete as it expands and contracts across seasons. And it resists hot-tire pickup, one of the most common failure points of epoxy floors in Arizona garages.

A 100% polyaspartic system means every layer — not just the topcoat — uses this chemistry. That's the standard we hold on every installation.

Owner on Every Job

Nick Bryditzki owns TRM Garage Floors and runs every installation personally. When you call for an estimate, you're talking to Nick. When the crew shows up, Nick is there. When the floor is done and it's time for the final walkthrough, Nick is still there. There are no subcontractors, no day crews, and no account managers between you and the person who did the work.

That accountability is deliberate. A floor coating is an investment in your home or business. The person installing it should be the same person who stands behind it.

What to Expect When You Work with TRM

The process starts with a free on-site estimate. Nick visits your property, assesses the concrete, explains your options, and gives you a straight price with no pressure and no obligation. If you decide to move forward, we schedule the install and show up on time. Surface preparation — diamond grinding, crack repair, pit fill — is completed before any coating touches the floor. The full polyaspartic system is applied and cured the same day. Nick walks the finished floor with you before leaving.

If something isn't right, you call Nick directly and it gets addressed. That's how it works.
