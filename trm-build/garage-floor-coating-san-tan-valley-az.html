Why San Tan Valley Homeowners Choose Polyaspartic

San Tan Valley's combination of newer construction and Pinal County's desert environment means concrete faces intense UV exposure and thermal cycling. Polyaspartic's UV stability and heat resistance make it the right system for this area.
