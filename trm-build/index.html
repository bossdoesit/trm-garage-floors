Sound Familiar?

Is Your Concrete Holding Your Space Back?

Bare concrete doesn't have to be the answer. Most of these problems are fixable in a single day with a professionally installed coating system.

🔴
Stained & Oily Floors

Oil, grease, and chemical stains soak into bare concrete permanently. A sealed polyaspartic surface wipes clean in seconds.

🔴
Peeling Epoxy

DIY epoxy kits and thin-coat systems peel, chip, and fail within a few years — especially in Arizona heat. Polyaspartic holds.

🔴
Hot-Tire Pickup

Hot tires bond to basic epoxy and pull the coating off when you back out. 100% polyaspartic is engineered to resist it.

🔴
Cracked & Dusty Concrete

Bare concrete dusts constantly and cracks spread over time. A coated floor seals the surface and makes cracks part of the prep, not the problem.

🔴
Slippery Patios

Smooth or faded outdoor concrete gets dangerously slick when wet. We apply slip-resistant finishes to every outdoor surface.

🔴
Worn Commercial Floors

High-traffic concrete deteriorates fast without a durable coating system. We resurface commercial spaces with fast return-to-service timing.

How It Works

Our Installation Process

Every TRM installation follows the same thorough process. No shortcuts, no skipped steps. Here's exactly what to expect.

1
Free Estimate

We visit your property, assess the concrete, discuss finish options, and provide a straight quote with no pressure and no obligation.

2
Surface Inspection

On install day we inspect the concrete for moisture, cracks, pitting, and contamination before any coating goes down.

3
Concrete Grinding

We diamond-grind the surface to open the concrete pores, remove contaminants, and create a profile for maximum coating adhesion.

4
Crack & Pit Repair

Cracks and pitting are filled and repaired before coating. Good prep is the foundation of a coating that lasts.

5
Base Coat Application

The polyaspartic base coat is applied to the prepared surface and allowed to cure to the right stage before the next layer.

6
Flake Broadcast

Decorative vinyl flakes are broadcast into the wet base coat to create texture, hide minor imperfections, and add visual depth.

7
Scrape & Clean

Excess flakes are scraped off and the surface is cleaned to create a smooth, even base for the topcoat.

8
Polyaspartic Topcoat

The final 100% polyaspartic topcoat seals everything in — protecting the flake layer and creating a durable, easy-clean surface.

9
Final Walkthrough

Nick walks the finished floor with you, answers any questions, and confirms you're satisfied before wrapping up.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is polyaspartic better than epoxy for garage floors?+
For most homeowners, a professionally installed polyaspartic garage floor coating is a better option than basic epoxy. Polyaspartic cures faster, resists UV yellowing, handles hot-tire pickup better, and creates a more durable surface when installed over properly prepared concrete. Unlike basic epoxy, 100% polyaspartic is UV stable and will not yellow or degrade under Arizona's intense sun exposure.
How long does a garage floor coating take to install?+
A professional polyaspartic garage floor coating is typically installed in one day. TRM Garage Floors arrives in the morning, completes surface preparation including grinding and crack repair, applies the full coating system, and completes a final walkthrough the same day. The floor is walk-on ready within hours and can handle vehicle traffic the following morning.
Can polyaspartic be used outside on patios and pool decks?+
Yes. Polyaspartic coatings are an excellent choice for outdoor concrete surfaces including patios, pool decks, walkways, and covered outdoor areas. Because polyaspartic is UV stable, it resists the fading and yellowing that affects epoxy-based coatings exposed to direct sunlight. We apply a slip-resistant finish to all outdoor surfaces.
How long does a polyaspartic garage floor coating last?+
A properly installed polyaspartic garage floor coating is designed to last many years with normal use and basic maintenance. The longevity depends on the quality of the surface preparation, the coating system used, and how the floor is maintained afterward. We install 100% polyaspartic systems — not thin-coat DIY products — which means the coating is substantially more durable than what you'd get from a hardware store kit.
How much does garage floor coating cost?+
Garage floor coating cost depends on the square footage of your floor, the condition of the concrete, any repairs needed, and the finish system you choose. TRM Garage Floors provides free, no-obligation estimates. Call (602) 301-8134 or submit a quote request online and we'll give you a straight number.
Do you install commercial floor coatings?+
Yes. TRM Garage Floors installs commercial floor coatings for warehouses, auto shops, retail showrooms, fire stations, workshops, and other high-traffic commercial spaces. Commercial applications often require coating systems that can handle heavier loads and higher foot traffic. We discuss the specific needs of your space during the estimate. See our Commercial Floor Coatings page for more information.
Do you resurface patios and decks?+
Yes. In addition to garage floor coating, TRM Garage Floors resurfaces patios, pool decks, covered outdoor areas, and other exterior concrete surfaces using our 100% polyaspartic system. See our Patio & Deck Resurfacing page for details.
