Garage Floor Coating Queen Creek FAQ

Is new construction concrete ready for coating in Queen Creek?+
Not immediately after the slab is poured. Concrete needs time to cure and off-gas moisture before a coating will bond correctly. We test concrete during the free estimate to confirm it's ready. Most new construction concrete in Queen Creek is ready for coating within the first year.
Do you coat garages in newer Queen Creek subdivisions?+
Yes. TRM Garage Floors works throughout Queen Creek's newer communities. If you're in a newer subdivision and want your garage floor coated, contact us for a free estimate.
