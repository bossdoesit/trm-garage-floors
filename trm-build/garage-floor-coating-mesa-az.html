Garage Floor Coating Mesa FAQ

Do you serve all of Mesa, AZ?+
Yes. TRM Garage Floors serves homeowners and businesses throughout Mesa — from central Mesa to Red Mountain and east Mesa communities. Contact us for a free estimate at any Mesa address.
Can you coat an older garage floor in Mesa?+
Yes. Diamond grinding during surface preparation handles most staining, old coatings, and surface contamination. Cracks and pitting are repaired before coating. Older concrete often benefits most from a professional coating because it addresses years of accumulated wear in a single day.
How long does polyaspartic last on a Mesa garage floor?+
A professionally installed 100% polyaspartic system is designed to last many years with normal use and basic maintenance. Polyaspartic holds up significantly better than DIY epoxy kits in Mesa's heat and UV environment.
