Garage Floor Coating Gilbert FAQ

How much does garage floor coating cost in Gilbert, AZ?+
Cost depends on the size of the floor, the condition of the concrete, and the finish system you choose. TRM Garage Floors offers free, no-obligation estimates for Gilbert homeowners. Call (602) 301-8134 or submit a request online.
How long does garage floor coating take in Gilbert?+
Most Gilbert garage floor coating jobs are completed in one day. We arrive, complete surface preparation including diamond grinding and crack repair, apply the full coating system, and do a final walkthrough the same day. Walk-on ready that evening, vehicle traffic the next morning.
Is polyaspartic coating better than epoxy for Gilbert, AZ garages?+
Yes, for most Gilbert homeowners. Polyaspartic is UV stable, so it won't yellow under Arizona's intense sun. It handles the high concrete temperatures Gilbert garages reach in summer without lifting or peeling. And it cures fast — enabling same-day installation without the multi-day wait of epoxy.
