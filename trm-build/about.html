TRM Garage Floors specializes in 100% polyaspartic coating systems for residential and commercial concrete. We serve homeowners and businesses across Gilbert, Mesa, Chandler, Queen Creek, San Tan Valley, Scottsdale, Apache Junction, Fountain Hills, Tempe, Sun Lakes, and Paradise Valley.

Why 100% Polyaspartic

We install polyaspartic because it's the right product for Arizona homeowners. UV stable, fast-curing, and significantly more durable than basic epoxy in extreme heat environments — polyaspartic delivers a floor that looks sharp and performs well for years. We don't cut corners by using an epoxy base coat under a polyaspartic topcoat. Every system we install is 100% polyaspartic from the concrete up.
