Commercial Floor Coating

What Are Commercial Floor Coatings?

Commercial floor coatings are professional-grade protective systems applied to concrete floors in commercial and industrial spaces. Unlike residential floors, commercial floors face higher foot traffic, heavier loads, chemical exposure from vehicles or equipment, and the operational cost of downtime during installation.

At TRM Garage Floors, we install 100% polyaspartic coating systems for commercial applications. The fast cure time of polyaspartic is particularly valuable in commercial settings — most spaces are back in service the following morning, minimizing disruption to your business operations.

Commercial Applications We Serve

  • Auto shops, dealerships, and service bays
  • Warehouses and distribution spaces
  • Retail showrooms and display floors
  • Fire stations and municipal facilities
  • Commercial garages and parking areas
  • Workshops and fabrication spaces
  • Office common areas with concrete floors
  • High-traffic entryways and corridors

Why Polyaspartic for Commercial Spaces

Commercial environments demand coatings that can take a beating. Heavy equipment, chemical spills, vehicle traffic, and constant foot traffic all wear down bare or poorly coated concrete fast. Polyaspartic bonds at the molecular level to prepared concrete and creates a surface that resists abrasion, chemical exposure, and impact — while remaining easy to clean and maintain.

The fast cure time also means commercial jobs don't require days of facility downtime. We arrive, prepare the surface, apply the full coating system, and the space is typically ready for vehicle traffic the following morning.

Common Questions

Commercial Floor Coating FAQ

How long does a commercial floor coating installation take?+
Most commercial floor coating jobs are completed in one day. The timeline depends on the square footage, the condition of the concrete, and any repairs needed. We'll give you a specific timeline during the free estimate so you can plan around your operations.
What types of commercial facilities do you serve?+
We serve auto shops, warehouses, retail showrooms, fire stations, commercial garages, workshops, offices with concrete floors, and other high-traffic commercial and industrial spaces. If your facility has concrete floors, contact us and we'll discuss the right coating system for your use case.
Can you coat a commercial floor while the business is operating?+
This depends on the size and layout of the space. For some commercial facilities, we're able to coat in sections to minimize disruption. For most jobs, the most efficient approach is to schedule installation during off-hours or a planned closure. We'll discuss the logistics during your free estimate.
How do commercial polyaspartic coatings compare to epoxy in a high-traffic environment?+
In high-traffic commercial environments, polyaspartic outperforms epoxy because it cures faster (reducing downtime), is more flexible under heavy loads and thermal cycling, and resists the UV exposure that causes epoxy to yellow. For commercial spaces where performance and minimal disruption matter, polyaspartic is the better system.
